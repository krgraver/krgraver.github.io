My story

I'm a UI/UX Designer living in Chicago. A marketing degree from Notre Dame got me started on the path of relating to users, but training at DESIGNATION gave me the tools to do so in my own unique way. I love anything that combines form with functionality and versatility; cars, watches, tools, and especially interfaces. I molded myself in the same way, learning UI, UX, and front-end development in order to one day call myself a full-stack designer.

I'm always pushing myself to learn something new, no matter the time or place. I want to work in a fast-paced environment where I can test and develop my design skills as much as possible. While my focus is in UI, I believe aptitude in UX and programming inform the best designs. To prove that point, I designed and coded everything you see on this site.
