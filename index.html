About Me

I'm a UI/UX Designer living in Chicago. A marketing degree from Notre Dame got me started on the path of relating to users, but training at DESIGNATION gave me the tools to do so in my own unique way. I love anything that combines form with functionality and versatility; cars, phones, shoes, and especially interfaces. I try to mold myself in the same way, learning visual design, user experience, and front-end development. I believe creativity makes life worth living, so I created everything you see on this site for you to enjoy.
