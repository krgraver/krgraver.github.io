LocalGuide

Client: DESIGNATION

Role: UX, UI, Branding

DESIGNATION provided an assignment for a travel app called LocalGuide that gives recommendations on what to do and see in different cities. My job was to create a primary persona to design for, develop wireframes, and make high fidelity mockups of the final product.

User interviews

I was given the transcripts of eight interviews where potential users were asked questions about previous travel experiences. After synthesizing all of the interview information, two personas emerged. However, one of them was largely an independent traveler who disliked travel guides and prefered to go to cities without much planning. Knowing this, I decided to focus on one primary persona:

Ariana
  • Ariana used to own her own bakery, but is now retired
  • When traveling, she wants to live and eat like a local
  • She likes personal accounts more than boring guidebooks
  • She needs to be able to keep track of interesting spots she finds while walking around

Wireframes

With Ariana in mind, I wireframed the screens for LocalGuide using Axure. My idea for the app was to create a community of reviewers who rate local attractions. This will help the app avoid the typical dryness of guidebooks and feel more personal. To encourage users to post reviews, there is a system of likes and reviewer statuses. Users can also save places they see and want to go to later in a wishlist.
